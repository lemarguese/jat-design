Health, Safety & Environment

At JAT Transport we believe that Safety and Operations are interlocked and this is our basis for all the work that we carry out. We continually strive for ZERO harm to people, property and the environment.

Before any cargo is handled or dispatched, health and safety hazards, risks and opportunities are identified, and assessed in accordance with our Quality processes and international standards.

All activities are thoroughly checked, prepared and assessed:

• Partners working on behalf of JAT Transport

• Previous incidents within the industry are analysed

• When applicable, any national or local law is complied

• Consequential Risks are assessed

We identify and assess key environmental risks and opportunities:

• all activities are evaluated, including ones that are outsourced to subcontractors on behalf of JAT Transportation

• previous incidents in the industry are also evaluated

• risks arising as direct consequences of natural disasters

JAT Transport has a robust sub-contract management process that ensures that expectations are understood and managed.
All risk assessments are discussed in detail to ensure that safe transportation is successfully delivered.

JAT Transport can provide expert supervision for loading and handling, as well as road surveys, loading plans, load restraints, oversize moves and heavy lift plans.
UN marked packaging materials are supplied for dangerous goods and repacked in accordance with IMDG/ADR/ICAO Dangerous goods standards.

We have many years of experience in the transportation and handling of oversized cargo. We conduct and develop route inspections that ensure the safe transit of goods.
Plans of lifting operations are drawn up and implemented in accordance with international standards.

All of our core activities get evaluated so that we can constantly expand, learn and get better as a team.
Thanks to permanent security of operations, we protect our customers by providing quality and safe services that help all of us to grow.

Our team has been trained in the internal quality system and its standards so we can assist you in ensuring safe and efficient operation.
