Services

We are engaged in international and local cargo transportation
in Kazakhstan, specialize in the transportation of equipment
and oversized cargo by sea, air, road and railroads. We also transport
dangerous goods, organize storage and loading works, help
with customs clearance and general consultations.

International Road
Transportation

Trailers can be positioned at suppliers premises within 2 x days of receiving
the booking at any location worldwide.

Standard Tent Trailers, Mega Trailers and Low bed trailers available
in Europe,Russia,China to Kazakhstan.

Often a popular choice in terms of price for high volume of cargo,
and middle range transit time.

Even 1x pallet can be sent by Road consolidation service from Europe
& China with average transit time 22x days.

Local Road Transportation
in Kazakhstan

For such a large country the size of Europe taking 7 x days from East to West,
you can rely on JAT to find you the trailer within a few hours of asking.

With over 12 x years operational experience delivering to remote locations around
the country, JAT has a reliable network of operators in each region offering
various types of trailers, flat beds from 12 upto 18 x meters.
прицепов, низкорамных грузовых прицепов-платформ от 12 до 18 метров.

We manage our drivers throughout the delivery process from loading, in transit
and unloading.

We ensure safe loading & securing practice as standard, and use of in-house HSE
consultant for cargo that is oversize or classed as heavy lift.

Seafreight

For those shipments from afar in North / South America and Asia, seafreight
is used and directed to either ports in the Baltics, Black Sea, and east coast
of China . Goods either continue in same container by rail or is transhipped and
reloaded to Rail owned containers/wagons. If time is of essence, then reloading
to trailers for door delivery service.

JAT Transport work with all known global container carriers and RORO operaters
for the oversize shipments.

For high volume freight tonnes, super oversize moves, chartering vessels is also
arranged via Europe through to the Russian River systems and into the Caspian
sea.

Airfreight

JAT Transport can arrange same day collection from anywhere in the world
and send your goods by passenger/cargo plane depending on the size
of your consignment.

For those crtical orders, dedicated air charter plane can be positioned
within 48-72 hours notice.

Will it fit?

  • All aircrafts have different capacities/allowances.
  • To ensure your cargo is suitable, just contact us for technical advice.
    ( md@jat.kz )
  • For shipments that are bigger than a shipping container - you must
    share transport drawings, indicating full dimensions, weights, lifting
    points, center of gravity.

Rail Freight

A practical freight option when there is high volume of freight and transit time
is not critical.

JAT can offer railfreight by standard 20’ and 40’ containers from
Europe,Russia,Caucuses, China into Kazakhstan.

For European cargo, below wagon sizes are coordinated out of Lodz, Poland
and Klaipeda,Lithuania.

Wagon 158м3 Cargo weight upto 66 tns

Wagon 220м3 Cargo weight upto 51 tns

Customs Clearance Services

JAT has experience with customs clearance services throughout the territory
of Kazakhstan via all modes of transport.

Full compliance and transparency with interpreting customs articles, HS codes
determination, Duties calculations and digital record management.

JAT Transport offers full turnkey service to include Customs clearance ontop
of your deliveries.

We offer customs advisory service to ensure your shipping documents
are compliant for Kazakhstan customs.
