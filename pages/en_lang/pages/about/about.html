About company

JAT Transport is a 100% locally registered Kazakh company with head office
based in Almaty. A specialist freight forwarder offering full range
of transportation services from Worldwide to Kazakhstan. Supporting
companies that serve major contracts within the Mining, Oil & Gas sectors.

We respect the vast competition in Kazakhstan, a country the size of Europe
with huge logistical challenges, yet confident in our own differentiators,
with local knowledge and international experience, its strategy and recipe
towards success, plus its committed passion for safety initiatives and introducing
value added services. Led by management team with over 25 years extensive
experience within Kazakhstan and Central Asia, driven by a passionate and smart
team that thrives on daily differing challenges and intent to deliver on customer
expectations

Company’s mission

JAT Transport aims to be your reliable business partner,
that offers value added services. Keeping it simple
with plenty of pro-active communication towards
understanding your business, your people and delivery
mission. JAT will constructively challenge your delivery
plans or projects through critical thinking and will offer
transport solutions with the aim of highlighting alternative
routings, cost effective delivery, improvement of transit
times

JAT will communicate with vendors worldwide and ensure packing requirements are suitable for the chosen mode of transport, shipping documents are vetted by our in house customs advisor before goods depart country of origin. With the topsy turvy times from financial crises, devaluations, pandemics, border closures, strikes, uprisings, sanctions – JAT Transport has helped companies navigate all setbacks to continually getting goods delivered to destinations. We ‘’get involved’’ with your suppliers.
