Local Road Transportation in Kazakhstan

For such a large country the size of Europe taking 7 x days from East to West, you can rely on JAT to find you the trailer within a few hours of asking. With over 12 x years operational experience delivering to remote locations around the country, JAT has a reliable network of operators in each region offering various types of trailers, flat beds from 12 upto 18 x meters. We manage our drivers throughout the delivery process from loading, in transit and unloading. We ensure safe loading & securing practice as standard, and use of in-house HSE consultant for cargo that is oversize or classed as heavy lift.

Seafreight

For those shipments from afar in North / South America and Asia, seafreight is used and directed to either ports in the Baltics, Black Sea, and east coast of China . Goods either continue in same container by rail or is transhipped and reloaded to Rail owned containers/wagons. If time is of essence, then reloading to trailers for door delivery service. JAT Transport work with all known global container carriers and RORO operaters for the oversize shipments. For high volume freight tonnes, super oversize moves, chartering vessels is also arranged via Europe through to the Russian River systems and into the Caspian sea.

Airfreight

JAT Transport can arrange same day collection from anywhere in the world and send your goods by passenger/cargo plane depending on the size of your consignment. For those crtical orders, dedicated air charter plane can be positioned within 48-72 hours notice.

Rail Freight

A practical freight option when there is high volume of freight and transit time is not critical. JAT can offer railfreight by standard 20’ and 40’ containers from Europe,Russia,Caucuses, China into Kazakhstan. For European cargo, below wagon sizes are coordinated out of Lodz, Poland and Klaipeda,Lithuania. Wagon 158 м3 Cargo weight upto 66 tns Wagon 220 м3 Cargo weight upto 51 tns

Customs Clearance Services

JAT has experience with customs clearance services throughout the territory of Kazakhstan via all modes of transport. Full compliance and transparency with interpreting customs articles, HS codes determination, Duties calculations and digital record management. JAT Transport offers full turnkey service to include Customs clearance ontop of your deliveries. We offer customs advisory service to ensure your shipping documents are compliant for Kazakhstan customs.
